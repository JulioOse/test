About the app

This app is designed to help you find and gather search results from various sources quickly. The sources that are used in the app are picked because of there credibility. Meaning that you don't have to comb through various search results on regular search engines to find credible ones.

How this app works

  1. First you select a category from the home page
  2. You then type the keyword that you are searching for in the search bar that is located on the top of the page
  3. Finally you click on one of the links below the search bar to take you to a page with the search word already entered
